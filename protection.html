Our protection measures against COVID-19

  • Each fitness equipments are separated with boards up to 2 meters tall.
  • Sterilizing tools are provided if gym users are desired to use.
  • Enhanced air-purifier and air-conditioning system can have up to 12 air exchange per hour.
  • We recommend our customers to follow CDC recommended steps if being infected:
    1. 1. Do not return to our centre until you meet the criteria to discontinue home isolation.
    2. 2. Talk with your healthcare provider about when it's safe for you to return to our centre.
